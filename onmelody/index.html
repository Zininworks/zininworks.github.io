OnMelody

Humming classical melody to SoundHound sucks

Application description

You've been there. A beautiful melody has come up in your head and keeps playing again and again. But you don't know what it is. You are frustrated because nobody recognizes your humming.

Good news for you. OnMelody is a search engine using the Musipedia database. You can identify the title and compose of a melody with just a few taps.
