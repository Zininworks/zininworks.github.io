Wordio

Collect New Word and Learn

ABOUT

Wordio is a simple and easy–to–use tool for collecting and learning words. Build your own vocabulary pack by adding a new word painlessly. Then, look it up on the fast and lightweight built–in dictionary with just a single tap. After you’ve learned the word, check it off to archive it. It is a very simple yet efficient way to improve your language skill.

FEATURES

Add New Word without Pain.

Copy a new word, swipe down, and tap. Add the word to your vocabulary pack directly, from anywhere using Wordio today widget.

Look It Up. Quickly.

iOS built–in dictionary is simple and very fast. It also works offline. And supports multiple languages.

Pull to Speak.

Slide to hear what the word sounds like. Pull more to speak slowly.

iCloud Sync.

Wordio keeps your word packs in sync across all your devices. Silent, swift, and smooth sync powered by CloudKit.
